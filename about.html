About MetaTech

Our Story

From 2005 to Today

MetaTech was founded in 2005 with a clear mandate: to bring world-class fiber optic and telecommunications infrastructure expertise to the rapidly growing Saudi market. Starting with a small team of specialist engineers, our early projects laid the backbone for what has become one of the Kingdom's most trusted names in OSP construction and fiber deployment.

Through the 2010s, we scaled our operations to serve major telecom operators, government entities, and large-scale real-estate developments. Our portfolio expanded from simple fiber pulls to complex multi-city backbone projects, FTTH rollouts covering tens of thousands of homes, and integrated civil and telecom infrastructure packages.

Today, MetaTech operates with over 300 completed projects, 200+ km of active fiber infrastructure, and a skilled workforce that continues to raise the bar for quality, safety, and on-time delivery in the region.

2005 Company Founded

Established in Riyadh with a core team of telecom engineers focused on OSP construction.

2009 First Major Operator Contract

Awarded first large-scale fiber backbone project with a tier-1 KSA telecom operator.

2013 FTTH Division Launch

Launched dedicated FTTH delivery unit to serve residential broadband expansion programs.

2017 ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 quality management certification, reinforcing our commitment to process excellence.

2021 O&M Services Expansion

Introduced structured 24/7 Operations & Maintenance services for enterprise and carrier clients.

2025 20 Years of Excellence

Celebrating two decades with 300+ completed projects and 200 KM+ of fiber infrastructure delivered.

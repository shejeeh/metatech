Who We Are

Engineering Excellence in Telecom Since 2005

MetaTech is a leading telecommunications and fiber optic infrastructure company established in 2005. With over two decades of field experience, we have built, maintained, and optimised thousands of kilometers of critical network infrastructure across Saudi Arabia.

Our teams deliver end-to-end solutions — from initial OSP survey and design through to full commissioning and long-term O&M — serving government agencies, tier-1 operators, and enterprise clients with the same rigorous standards.

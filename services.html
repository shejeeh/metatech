What We Offer

Our Services

End-to-end telecommunications and fiber optic infrastructure services — engineered for performance, built for reliability.

01
OSP

OSP Design & Construction

Our Outside Plant (OSP) engineering team handles the full lifecycle of telecom infrastructure — from initial route survey and geospatial mapping to civil construction, duct installation, pit & manhole construction, and cable placement.

  • Route surveying and topographic mapping
  • Permit acquisition and authority coordination
  • Duct bank and conduit system design
  • Manhole, handhole, and cable vault construction
  • Directional drilling and micro-trenching
  • As-built documentation and GIS data delivery
02
Fiber

Fiber Optic Installation

Precision deployment of single-mode (SM) and multi-mode (MM) fiber optic cables across underground, aerial, and direct-buried configurations. Our certified splicers and installation crews ensure signal integrity from end to end.

  • Underground and buried fiber cable pulling
  • Aerial lashing and OPGW installation
  • Fiber splicing — fusion and mechanical
  • Splice closure installation and sealing
  • Fiber distribution hub and ODF termination
  • Cable labelling, documentation and handover
03
FTTH

FTTH Networks

Complete Fiber-to-the-Home passive optical network (PON) design and deployment for residential communities and mixed-use developments. We design scalable PON topologies and deliver subscriber-ready connections with full testing and acceptance.

  • PON architecture design (GPON / XGS-PON)
  • Passive splitter cabinet and pit installation
  • Drop cable routing and subscriber termination
  • In-building wiring and ONT mounting
  • Feeder, distribution, and drop cable management
  • Customer acceptance testing and handover
04
Testing

Testing & Commissioning

Rigorous, standards-compliant optical testing across newly installed and in-service networks. We use industry-leading OTDR, OLTS, and inspection equipment to verify every fiber link meets specification before acceptance.

  • OTDR trace analysis — event detection and loss budgeting
  • Optical loss (insertion loss) testing with OLTS
  • Connector end-face inspection (400× microscopy)
  • Optical return loss (ORL) measurement
  • Power level verification and link budget validation
  • Comprehensive test report generation and archiving
05
O&M

Operation & Maintenance

Long-term network health through structured preventive and corrective maintenance programs. Our 24/7 response capability ensures minimal downtime and maximum network availability for critical infrastructure clients.

  • 24/7 NOC monitoring and fault response
  • Fiber fault localisation and emergency restoration
  • Scheduled preventive maintenance inspections
  • Splice closure cleaning and re-sealing
  • Network performance trend reporting
  • SLA-based service agreements
06
Civil

Civil & Infrastructure Work

The physical backbone of every network begins with quality civil work. MetaTech's civil teams are experienced in all aspects of telecom civil construction — executed safely and to municipal authority standards across KSA.

  • Open trenching and backfilling
  • Horizontal directional drilling (HDD)
  • Concrete duct bank and cable tray installation
  • Manhole and handhole construction and fitting
  • Road reinstatement and surface restoration
  • Building entry and riser infrastructure preparation
